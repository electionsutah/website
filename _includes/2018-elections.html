Election Deadlines
  •   March 15, 2018
    Declaration of candidacy period ends; declaration of intent to gather signatures period ends
  •   March 26, 2018
    Financial disclosure report due for unaffiliated legislative candidates
  •   May 15, 2018
    Financial disclosure report due for unaffiliated state board of education candidates
  •   June 5, 2018
    Primary election mail ballots are sent to voters
  • June 19, 2018
    Primary election in-person early voting begins
  • June 22, 2018
    Primary election in-person early voting ends (a county may extend early voting until June 25th)
