Elections Deadlines

Presidential Primary Election: March 3, 2020

  •   January 17, 2020
    Ballots are transmitted to military voters, their dependents, and voters living outside of the U.S.
  •   February 3, 2020
    Last day to mail a paper voter registration form.
  •   February 11-18, 2020
    Mail ballots are sent to voters.
  •   February 18, 2020
    In-person early voting begins. You may register to vote at an early voting location by casting a provisional ballot.
  •   February 25, 2020
    Last day to request a mail ballot. It is also the last day to register to vote using the online registration system or at your county clerk’s office.
  •   February 28, 2020
    In-person early voting ends in some jurisdictions, but your jurisdiction may extend the early voting period until March 2nd.
  •   March 3, 2020
    Primary election in-person voting

Regular Primary Election – June 30, 2020

Regular General Election – November 3, 2020
